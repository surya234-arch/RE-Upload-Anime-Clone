Berserk

“Even if we painstakingly piece together something lost, it doesn’t mean things will ever go back to how they were.”

⚔️ Genre: Dark Fantasy, Action, Psychological, Tragedy

📅 Seasons: 3 main adaptations

🎞️ Movies: 3 (Golden Age Arc trilogy)

⭐ Rating: 9.1 / 10

Berserk follows Guts, a lone mercenary with a massive sword and a tragic past. Haunted by destiny and betrayal, he walks a blood-soaked path seeking vengeance against his former friend Griffith — now a demonic god-hand. This anime defines dark fantasy with unmatched brutality and emotion. 🩸
